One thing always remains the same in the fitness world, consistency pays off!

What do I do?:

I have been working as qualified personal trainer for the last 10 years. In that time, I have continuously pushed myself to develop. I am committed to improving myself and my knowledge for my clients. As a trainer, I work with my clients to find what works best for them. This means working out a training and nutrition schedule that lends itself to their current lifestyle and not enforcing one. Listening and adapting to each clients enables me to give a truely personalised experience to each individual. Having worked in the fitness industry for this length of time, I have seen plenty of trends come and go. However, one thing has remained the same, consistency pays off!

Continuous Professional Development:

I attend as many training courses as possible on topics including hypertrophy, female specific training and sport specific training. I have also completed formal training with a masters in nutrition and training from NUI Galway in 2015. While looking for a new challenge after my masters, I took up golf myself in 2016. Seeing the demands that the sport places on the body, I gained TPI® level 3 qualification. I've used this to deliver seminars and promote mobility in all my clients.

Instead of being part of the cure, maybe I could be part of the prevention?

My background:

Prior to becoming a personal trainer, I worked as a Radiation Therapist. Although I loved my job, I wanted to see if I could help people in a different way. Instead of being part of the cure, maybe I could be part of the prevention?

Practicalities:

If you wish to train with me I offer both online and face-to-face options. When training face-to-face you can join me at the Irish Strength Institute's Centre of Excellence in Malahide Marina. The facility is open Monday-Friday 6am-10pm and Saturdays from 8am-5pm. As this is a provate facility for personal training and small group training only, times outside of this can be accommodated

The faciliy at Malahide Marina also boasts a Sauna and Floatation Therapy suite. My clients can avail of these at a reduced price.
